THE PROCESS OF COLLECTION

The most important of all things is the art of noticing. Every thing around you is worth noticing. Everything around you holds a story. Journalling is telling a story about yourself. It's your time capsule. Amidst the hustle and bustle of adapting to a new environment, I found solace in journalling. Started as a hobby in 2020, this grew up to be one of my most successful passions ever. During lockdown it was just counting days and finding joy in the simple things. I realised that change is something that is not to be feared but to be embraced. It pushes us out of our comfort zones, forces us to grow and opens doors we never knew existed. The idea of creating a portfolio induced me to learn HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

MY STYLE

I intend to create my works using TOMBOW Brush Pens, STAEDLER Fine Liners, ZEBRA Midliners, SAKURA Micron Pens and ZEBRA SARASA Fine Liners. It is intended to give a touch of personality for each person. Upon request I do create personalised planners, mood boards, to-do lists, trackers, to-be-read lists and much more, (the list is available in my catalogue). I can also create personality based planning structure as well. Overall, my usage involves a scrapbook style planning method adorned with washi tapes, vellum sheets, scrap paper, stickers, film stickers and transparent sheets. You give us the style, we provide it for you.
